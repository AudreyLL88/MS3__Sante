It's Apéro time!

L'Apé-what? L'apéro in french is an after-work drink that you share with your co-worker. As we are all working from home now and the bars are closed, It is important to learn to socialize in a different way. With Santé! No need for a bartender, make your own drink and call your friends from the safety and comfort of your own home!
